Data Sources

Wage data is drawn from the Office of Foreign Labor Certification (OFLC) prevailing wage database, cross-referenced with Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics (OEWS) survey data published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. Data files can be found here.

Geographic boundaries use the US Atlas TopoJSON county dataset. Occupation titles follow the Standard Occupational Classification (SOC) system.

Data covers a 3-year merged period. Some counties may have limited or no coverage for specific occupations.
